FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can I switch plans later?
Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime from your account; we prorate the difference.
What happens at the end of my free trial?
Your account stays active in read-only mode until you pick a plan. Your saved jobs and brand profile stay put.
What counts as a "quote"?
Each time you save a new job (not a draft) it counts as a quote for your monthly limit. Editing an already-saved job is free.
Do you charge for the AI plan-reader separately?
AI usage is included with Pro and Unlimited up to your monthly quote limit.
Is there a setup fee?
No. Sign up, pick a plan, start quoting.
Can I get a refund?
Yes — within 14 days of your first paid charge, no questions asked.
Is there a contract?
No. Cancel anytime; you keep access through the end of your billing cycle.
